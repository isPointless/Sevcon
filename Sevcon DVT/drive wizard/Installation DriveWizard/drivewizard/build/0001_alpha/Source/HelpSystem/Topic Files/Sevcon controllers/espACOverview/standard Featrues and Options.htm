Standard features and capabilities


Available options
There are two mechanical package options (Figure 2) for the espAC controller at each voltage and current rating. In self-cooled fromat, espAC operates built-in fans to control unit temperature. You can configure whether the fans operate on a full-time or on-demand basis.

Self-cooled frame Base-plate cooled frame
Figure 2 Mechanical package options
For either package espAC is fully protected against short-term and long-term overloads by a function which temporarily reduces the maximum allowed torque demand when the power frame temperature or the power MOSFET junction temperatures reach the maximum safe limits for the operating conditions of torque and speed.


Intended use of the espAC
The espAC motor controller can be used in any of these main applications for both pump and traction control:
• Counterbalanced, warehouse and pedestrian fork lift trucks
(Classes 1 to 3, FLT1, 2 & 3)
• Airport ground support (AGS), incluing tow tractors
• Utility vehicles
• Burden carriers
• Sweepers and scrubbers
• Golf buggies/carts
• Neighborhood electric vehicles (NEV)
• Scooters
• Marine
